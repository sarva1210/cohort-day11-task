🌴 Fun & Cool Facts About Beaches

1. Singing Sand Beaches Exist Some beaches have "singing sand" that makes a squeaky or musical sound when you walk on it (e.g., Whistling Sands in Wales).

2. Beaches Can Be Different Colors * Not all beaches are white! * 🌋 **Black sand** beaches exist in Hawaii (from volcanic lava). * 🌈 **Pink sand** beaches can be found in Bermuda and the Bahamas. * 🌲 **Green sand** beaches exist in Hawaii due to a mineral called olivine.

3. Beaches Move Over Time * Sand on a beach is constantly shifting — waves, wind, and currents move it around. A beach you visit this year might look different next year!
